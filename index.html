About PostureMom

For over 5 years, my brother and I have had a monthly tradition: order some take-out, pick up some beers, and watch a bad movie. And with this tradition, the worse the movie, the better our movie night. It was a way to kick back and unwind.

But finding the best bad movie isn't easy. So I decided to make PostureMom.

With BMN, all you need to do is fill out the form to help you find the bad movie you love, and it'll search the bad movie database and find you the perfect movie to unwind with your loved one.
